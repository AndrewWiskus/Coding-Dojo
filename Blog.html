Work Projects

I have had the opportunity to build some amazing aircraft in my position as head of special projects. However, none were as incredible as the very first airplane I ever built. I was fortunate enough to be one of the head builders on this Waco Biplane. The build took one year to complete and is one of the most unique aircraft to ever fly. It is built on a 1939 Waco frame with a 450 horespower radial engine as well as a jet engine mounted to the belly of the airplane. We were commissioned by Jack Links to make something truly special and I believe we delivered.
